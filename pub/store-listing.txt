Full description:
This application adds Grand River Transit buses information to MonTransit BETA.

This app contains the buses schedule (available offline) and the real-time next departures from http://realtimemap.grt.ca as well as the latest news from @GRT_ROW on Twitter.

BETA: Thanks for your patience while the last features are being added and the last bugs are being fixed.

Grand River Transit serves Waterloo, Cambridge, Kitchener, St. Jacobs and Elmira in Ontario, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit BETA app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit BETA app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Region of Waterloo and Grand River Transit.
http://www.regionofwaterloo.ca/en/regionalGovernment/GRT_GTFSdata.asp

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-grand-river-transit-bus-android

This app is not related with the Region of Waterloo and Grand River Transit.

Permissions:
- Other: required to load real-time schedule and read news (Twitter)
